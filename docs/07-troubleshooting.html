7. Troubleshooting

No Sound

  1. Check R3 firmware upload.
  2. Verify BCLK, WS, and DOUT wiring.
  3. Verify SD/EN behavior for your DAC board.

Only Sine Tone

Disable TEST_TONE and upload again.

Distortion

Reduce AUDIO_MASTER_GAIN_PERCENT in platformio.ini.

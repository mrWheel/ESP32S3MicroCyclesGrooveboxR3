Velocity (VEL)

Range:


1 - 127

Velocity controls impact.

Value What You Hear
1-20 Almost silent
21-50 Soft hit
51-90 Normal hit
91-120 Strong hit
121-127 Maximum attack

Example:


127 80 115 70

This sounds more human than:


127 127 127 127

2. Hardware and Wiring (R3)

Audio Pins

  • BCLK: GPIO26
  • WS/LRCLK: GPIO25
  • DOUT: GPIO27
  • SD/EN: GPIO14

Audio Wiring Checklist

  1. ESP32 BCLK to DAC BCLK
  2. ESP32 WS to DAC WS/LRC
  3. ESP32 DOUT to DAC DIN
  4. Shared GND

If You Hear Nothing

Check SD/EN behavior for your DAC board and verify speaker power path.

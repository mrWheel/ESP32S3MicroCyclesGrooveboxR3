Trigger Page (TRIG)

The Trigger page decides when sounds play.

Example:


X...X...X...X...

Meaning:


1
5
9
13

What you hear:

  • More triggers = busier rhythm
  • Fewer triggers = more space

Good beginner pattern:


KICK  X...X...X...X...
SNARE ....X.......X...
CH    X.X.X.X.X.X.X.X.
